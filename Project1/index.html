about

This project looks at the collection of ways animals move together: flocks, herds, pods, and schools, and how that movement holds a kind of quiet beauty. Watching them, you can sense how deeply they depend on one another, how their rhythms overlap and form a living pattern. Through images, sounds, and small fragments of information, I want this project to reflect how lives move together and how they are all connected.
